### 证明lemma2
#### 源代码
```coq
Lemma ex2 : forall A B : Prop, A \/ B -> ~ (~A /\ ~B).
Proof.
  intro h1.
  intro h2.
  intro h3.
  intro h4.
  destruct h4 as (h41 , h42).
  destruct h3 as [h31 | h32].
  - apply h41. assumption.
  - apply h42. assumption.
Qed.

```
